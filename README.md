Title: EasyMED-Because it's easy and convenient.\n
Overview: HealthConnect is a comprehensive digital healthcare platform designed to streamline patient access to medical services. It integrates appointment booking, medicine purchasing, hospital location, and cognitive testing into a single user-friendly interface, improving convenience and promoting proactive health management.
Problem Statement: Accessing healthcare services efficiently is a persistent challenge for many individuals. Patients often face difficulties in scheduling timely appointments, locating nearby hospitals, and obtaining prescribed medicines, leading to delays in treatment and increased stress. Additionally, cognitive health monitoring is rarely integrated into everyday healthcare, making early detection of cognitive decline difficult. There is a clear need for a unified digital platform that allows users to manage their healthcare seamlessly—enabling appointment booking, medicine purchase, hospital navigation, and cognitive assessment—all in one accessible and user-friendly system.
Approach and solution: The project follows a user-centric approach, focusing on accessibility, convenience, and integration of multiple healthcare services.Users can search for doctors by specialty, location, and availability.Users can upload prescriptions and order medicines securely.Interactive maps help users find nearby hospitals and clinics.Provides interactive tests for memory, attention, and problem-solving.
